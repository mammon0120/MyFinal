*****Game Rules*****
1. Starting Point: Players start at the starting point with a HP(Health Points) of 100.
2. Rolling the Dice: The number of steps players can move is determined by the dice.
3. Rewards: Along the way, players may meet rewards squares.
4. Penalty: There are also penalty squares on the path.
5. Game Win: Successfully reaching the endpoint. The board is 30 squares long.
6. Game Over: Lose the game if the HP drops to 0 before reaching the endpoint.
